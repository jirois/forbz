        • Why Forbes?

          There are variety of reasons to include hedge funds in a portfolio of otherwise traditional investments. The most cited reason to include them in any portfolio is their ability to reduce risk and add diversification. We have mentioned before how many hedge funds claim absolute return mandates whereby returns are minimally correlated with equity market. In such case, hedge funds serve as a great means to diversify, particularly in times of increased market volatility and/or an outright bear market.
